Publicis Remote Part-Time Cooperation Agreement

Welcome to join the remote part-time task platform under Publicis Groupe. To protect the rights of both parties and clarify the cooperation relationship, the following agreement is hereby signed:

Article 1: Purpose

This contract is intended to regulate the behavior of part-time personnel in performing remote tasks, ensuring tasks are completed to standard and commissions are paid on time.

Article 2: Scope of Cooperation

Part-time personnel register an account through the platform and complete simulated order tasks such as placing orders and writing reviews. Commissions are automatically settled upon completion.

Article 3: Remuneration

After completing a task group, the platform settles the payment to the users Ethereum wallet address within 24 hours. Users must provide a valid ETH wallet address.

Article 4: Confidentiality

Users shall not disclose platform task details or reward structure, nor transfer their accounts to others.

Article 5: Termination

If users violate platform rules or provide false information, the platform has the right to terminate cooperation and freeze commissions.

Article 6: Miscellaneous

This agreement is effective upon online confirmation and has legal effect. In case of disputes, both parties shall negotiate amicably.
